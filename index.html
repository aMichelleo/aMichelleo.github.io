There are many types of cows, each with their own unique qualities. It is important to educate yourself on the different cows of the world.

  • Highland cows

Dangers of Cows

Cows produce a lot of methane which is bad for the earth. People also eat cows in the form of beef which can give you salmonella and will make you sick if not cooked properly. If you feed your cows other cows, they will also give you mad cow disease.
