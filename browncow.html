Angus Cattle

Angus cows are cows are one of the most scary looking cows. They are built really big and look like they could easily trample a small animal. They usually weigh between 1870-1210 pounds. Even with their rather terrifying build, they are the most common breed of beef cattle in America.

Characteristics of the Angus Cow

Baby Angus cows, in contrast to their full grown form, are absoultey adorable. They are almost always black, and the bulls are know for being particularly aggressive.

What to know

  • They are not even remotely endangered
  • They originated in scotland
  • They are produced for their meat. Their beef is thought to be superior.
